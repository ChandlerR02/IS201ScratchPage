About Willy Adames

Willy Adames is a professional baseball player known for his skills as a shortstop. Born on September 2, 1995, in Santiago, Dominican Republic, Adames has become a fan favorite for his incredible defense, clutch hitting, and energetic personality. Willy Adames just recently got traded to the San Francisco Giants.
